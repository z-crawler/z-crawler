Job Description

Specialists Hospital of Shreveport, a leader in advanced technology in the fields of orthopedic & spine surgery, pain management and physical therapy, is seeking experienced Inpatient Med/Surg RNs to join our team.  This position is a full time position on a rotating 12 hour shift cycle.

To be considered, applicants must be a licensed RN in the state of Louisiana with a minimum of two years of recent inpatient care experience (orthopedic specialty preferred) and have current BCLS & ACLS.  Professional organization membership encouraged.  Candidates must be available for night shifts, able to perform CPR, manage physical activities including extensive standing, moderated sitting, mobile patient devices & direct patient transfer with assistance.


Duties include:  patient data collection & interpretation, the recording & communication of relevant data to the medical staff, planning post-operative care, direct patient care, post-surgical evaluation and assessment, direct interaction with patients & family members, nursing peers, physicians as well as other departmental staff.

Benefits include a comprehensive medical, vision, dental with other supplemental plans also available, vacation & PTO time available based on time in service, and other programs. For consideration, please submit a resume with cover letter to [Click Here to Email Your Resumé].  No calls or walk-ins please.  Specialists Hospital is an equal opportunity employer.
