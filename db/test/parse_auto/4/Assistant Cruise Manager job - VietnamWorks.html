        Assistant Cruise Manager

        Assistant Cruise Manager

        Paradise Cruises
        Tuan Chau Island

        Save job | Add job alert for similar jobs

        Job Description

        • Ensure effective communication within the department and/ or with other departments
        • Liaise with all departments regarding guest information, special requests, VIP’s, weekly operations i.e. room status, package meals, VIP’s etc.
        • Ensuring guest feedback passed on to the associates promptly
        • Greeting and speaking with all passengers, especially VIP’s guest, during their stay on board
        • To ensure all guest requests are passed to the right persons, personally speaking to guests in event of problems
        • Action or pass to the right the guest preferences, feedback etc.
        • Assist Manager in controlling all departmental expenses, daily beverage order list and submit to the Purchasing Manager/Store man for central Assist in ordering and controlling equipment.
        • Support Manager in performing and controlling the efficient and effective operations with Paradise Cruises SOPs from Front office, Dining room and Housekeeping
        • Liaise with Cruise Operations to get daily passenger status, VIPs, special requirements
        • Collect daily guest comment and hand them to Service Quality & Customer Satisfaction department and follow up to deal the guest comments/ complaints to give appropriate feedback if required
        • Check to coordinate with the right person to ensure all equipments in good conditions
        • Conduct monthly inventories, damages report
        • Ensure the hygiene standards by conducting regular surveys on cleaning procedures
        • Be in charge of set up work schedule for all staff onboard
        • Report and coordinate with HR. dept. for the staff recruitment and termination
        • Report any offences/ warning of staff members to deal with measures set by company policies and regulations
        • Observe and monitor performance of staff members to propose the promotion
        • Perform other duties as assigned by the management.

        Job Requirement

        • Experience in hospitality or tourism
        • Excellent both Oral & Verbal in English, knowledge of second main language is an advantage,
        • Computer – literate
        • Strong knowledge in Food and Beverage, Front office and Housekeeping
        • Strong organization and team leader skills, can work under high pressure
        • Strong team player with a “can – do” attitude
        • Hospitality and independently in work
        • Expatriate and/ or Vietnamese
        • Degree in Hospitality or Tourism Management
