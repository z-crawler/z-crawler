        PHP Developer Of Ad Ebis, Web App Of Internet Ad Measurement System

        PHP Developer Of Ad Ebis, Web App Of Internet Ad Measurement System

        Lockon Vietnam Co.,ltd.
        Zen Plaza, 54-56 Nguyen Trai, Dist. 1, HCMC

        Save job | Add job alert for similar jobs

        Job Description

        【Summary】
        We're still just a start-up company in Vietnam. We develop AD EBiS, No.1 Internet ad measurement web-based system in Japan, with the parent company in Japan as 1 team.

        You will aim at "Impact On The World" with us, developing valuable products as a PHP developer.　We're hiring only 2 developers at this time.

        【How you work?】
        You will participate in a whole software development project.
        - Develop new features or maintain existing features of AD EBiS mainly on its admin screen and batch system.
        - Develop some inhouse systems for the company.
        - Develop the other products of us or EC site with EC-CUBE(in the future).

        Your responsibility as a developer at Lockon Vietnam is really big and important. You will do a lot of things like the following :
        - Research effects by upgrading
        - Write spec documents
        - Read existing code and write code
        - Design unit test cases
        - Run unit tests
        - Design integration test cases
        - Run integration tests
        - Report bugs
        - Fix bugs
        etc.

        By the way, don't you have experience in writing documents or test cases? Don't worry about that, we know it.
        We have a helpful training program to guide you. You can gain not only coding skill but also a various kind of skills as a tremendous software engineer at Lockon Vietnam.

        【References】
        - Programming language: PHP (plain PHP, with CakePHP)
        - Communication tool: Skype, Google hangout or TV conference system for video chat
        - Communication language with JP team: English
        (The communicator will support you anytime.)
        - Bug tracking tool: Redmine
        - Source management tool: SVN
        - Automation testing tool: Selenium
        - Development process: Waterfall (basically)

        Job Requirement

        【Requirement】
        ★ Necessary Behavior
        - Read, read, read a lot of good code, learn from others, steal better ways from others.
        - Pay attention to not only the result but also the process and operation.
        - Be positive, be honest, don't hesitate to say anything you think needed for the team.

        ★ WORK EXPERIENCE
        - More than 1 year WORK EXPERIENCE AFTER GRADUATION as a PHP developer

        - Experience in pure PHP and typical PHP framework (especially, CakePHP framework) will help you
        - Experience in Smarty will help you
        - Experience in popular DBMS like Postgres SQL, MySQL etc. will help you
        - Understanding about software development processes
        - Understanding about object-oriented programming

        ★ COMPETENCIES/SKILLS
        - Logical thinking and good problem-solving skill
        - Positive thinking and spirit of challenge
        - Ability to read documents written in English and write documents in English

        【Environment】
        - Team building is really important. Company activities like picnics and parties are held often.
        - Active, friendly, motivated and supportive colleagues
        - Easy to suggest your idea to improve the company
        - Challenging development with advanced technology
        - Acquisition of the knowledge related to advanced online marketing
        - Exciting experience to build the new company and the development team
        - Young and friendly bosses
        - Feeling close to friendly and reliable JP members (connected with TV conference system)
        - Training course for new developers
        - Study meeting by a technical specialist (both from Lockon JP/VN)

        【Other information】
        - 8:00 - 17:30 (1:30 lunch break), 5 days from Monday to Friday
        - Twice salary appraisal a year(April and October)
        - Evaluated based on the performance at projects you joined and other activities that contribute to make the company better
        - Basic Tet bonus (paid 2 times separately. 50% on July, 50% on January)
        - 100% salary of probation period
        - Insurance : SI, HI and UI as defined in the Labor law.
        - Annual company trip
        - A company outdoor activity for team building about once a month

        【Recruitment process】
        We'll send you our company introduction document via Email when we invite you to 1st interview. We'd like you to pass 2 interviews and 1 technical test in our recruitment process.

        ★Notice★
        May we ask what your career objective is?
        - To be a good staff? become a team leader?
        - Have a good opportunity to get promoted in your job?
        - Try to learn as much as possible and doing your best?
        If you write like that in your CV, we would recommend you to consider about what you really want to do through your work and rewrite the objectives by YOUR OWN WORDS before applying for us. Besides, please confirm the requirements including salary range carefully.

        Finally, we will praise highly people who put their hand up and try a new approach. We are willing to support that. You don’t limit your capability by yourself. You can learn hungrily from others. You can not only do what you have to do, but also realize what you want to do with your teammates at Lockon Vietnam.

        We are looking forward to application from you, thank you very much for reading!
