        Marketing Manager ( Urgent )

        Marketing Manager ( Urgent )

        Puratos Grand-place Vietnam (PGPV)
        HCMC Office: Room C4 & Room C1, 1A Me Linh Square, District 1, HCMC

        Save job | Add job alert for similar jobs

        Job Description

        Job summary:
        • Accelerate the company’s growth and achieve the budget turnover through establishing and executing marketing programs which directly contribute to sales, brand recognition and customer satisfaction.
        • Marketing Manager is responsible for assisting the General Manager in defining & implementing the marketing strategy of the subsidiary in terms of new developments i.e. new products, new markets and/or new customers optimizing the performance of the sales team through promotions, samples, brochures, information, training and technical support, public relations and communication activities
        • Report to: Managing Director

        Core tasks:
        - Assist to develop an annual business plan (budget) and contribute to achieve targets.
        - Plan and execute an annual marketing plan tailored to customer category.
        - Following the priority of focus products, plan and execute intensive promotional campaigns.
        - Co-ordinate new product development, launch and follow-up.
        - Develop and execute a cost-effective annual communications plan including PR and advertising.
        - Establish the information flow of products and services through regular newsletters, new releases and whatever effective form of communication.
        - Develop and produce cost-effectively sales tools: collateral, visual and written materials. Ensure these to be distributed through the right internal/external channels.
        - Make sure marketing budget expenses are under control.
        - Plan and organize exhibitions
        - Assist in preparing and presenting concepts to Key Accounts with Sales team.
        - Conduct market surveys to support and understand consumer taste and perception in the local Vietnam market.
        - Establish and maintain database of customers.
        - Co-ordinate with internal and external parties for product development and customer service to achieve overall company goals.
        - Maintain monthly reporting to the Regional marketing manager, General Manager in charge of Sales and Marketing division

        Job Requirement

        - A degree in business administration or marketing
        - At least 5 years of working experience in a marketing capacity – Trade Marketing, FMCG, or research background preferred
        - Fluent in spoken and written English
        - Proficiency in MS Office, especially power point and excel
        - Excellent Communication and presentation skill
        - Team player, hardworking and result oriented
