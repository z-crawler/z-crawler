Company Overview

Olive Garden
Olive Garden is a family of local restaurants that is the leader in the highly competitive Italian segment of casual dining. The company currently operates over 800 restaurants in the United States and Canada. We employ over 96,000 and our sales exceed $3.6 Billion annually. Olive Garden is a subsidiary of Darden Restaurants, Inc. headquartered in Orlando, Florida.
