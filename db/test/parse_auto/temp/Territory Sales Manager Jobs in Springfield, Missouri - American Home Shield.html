Territory Sales Manager
American Home Shield | Springfield, MO | 8/14/2014
JOB DESCRIPTION

ServiceMaster/American Home Shield is currently hiring for a Territory Sales Manager within their HSA (Home Security of America, Inc.) home warranty brand.

Summary: The Territory Manager represents Home Security of America to our clients and customers. The Territory Manager is responsible for maintaining and building the business in the assigned region.

 

Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.

Present HSA’s message to individual agents and brokers, as part of maintaining existing relationships as well as prospecting new clients.

 

Develop presentations to target top-producing agents and secure commitment. Follow up with commitments and monitor percentage attainment of stated goals.

 

Make office visits on pre-determined schedule to maintain visibility and strength existing relationships within territory. 

 

Responsible for training presentations, sales meetings, lunch & learns, realtor board events, open houses, and other similar events.

 

Responsible for maintaining inventory of marketing supplies and warranty brochures for all offices.

 

Answer and return telephone calls within two hours of the call or, if in meetings, return calls within an absolute minimum of four hours.

 

Maintain marketing concessions and monitor their use judiciously.  Adhere to contract and call center policies.

 

Adhere to necessary systems to maintain calendar, contacts, and clients; expense reporting; and qualifications of clients/leads, daily.

 

Follow up on all service issues with personal contact to reinforce our value proposition and make sure the issue has been resolved in a satisfactory manner.

 

Keep appropriate Regional Director or Regional Vice President informed of key issues. 

Manage prescribed budget. 

 

Demonstrate a mastery of HSA programs and services.  Demonstrate knowledge of competitor’s products and services and know who the field reps are and the key points of difference. 

 

Supervisory Responsibilities

 

In conjunction with the Regional Director or Regional Vice President, the Territory Manager will supervise and manage Service Representatives, providing leadership in account management and customer service.


JOB REQUIREMENTS

Qualifications To perform the job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily.  The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.  Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

Education and/or Experience

Bachelor’s degree (B.A.) from four-year College or university; or four years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.


Computer Skills

To perform this job successfully, an individual should have knowledge of Gmail, net software, Microsoft Office Suite, Virtus, OWN, Francis, and typing of 30 WPM or passing a keyboarding class.


Certificates, Licenses, Registrations

Participate in social media groups, Interact with members of the real estate community through various communication mediums, i.e. local Board of Realtors, WCR; all to be discussed with Regional Director.  


Other Skills and Abilities

Completion of HSA’s current training program; Completion of the Toastmaster Program preferred


Physical Demands

The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job.  Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
